Cencheng Shen received the BS degree in Quantitative Finance from National University of Singapore in 2010, and the PhD degree in Applied Mathematics and Statistics from Johns Hopkins University in 2015. He worked as a visiting scholar in the Department of Statistical Science in Temple University from 2015 to 2016, and re-joined Johns Hopkins University as a research scientist in The Center for Imaging Science since 2016. His research interest is focused on various aspects of multi-modal data, including testing independence, correlation analysis, kernel transformation, dimension reduction and manifold learning, pattern recognition and supervised learning.
